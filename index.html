Who are we?

Brainwash is a laundrette and cafe. Providing events from Open Mic Nights to Live Music.

Welcome to our website, here you can reserve a table as well as book washers and dryer. Here you can find the latest event information and browse our refreshments.

Where are we?

Brainwash is located at 38 Park St. in Bristol, our postcode is BS1 5JG.
Using the provided map you can get a better idea - we look forward to seeing you!
